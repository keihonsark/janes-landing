Safe Return Home

Thoughtful Support After a Hospital Stay

Nearly 1 in 5 patients experience an adverse event within 3 weeks of leaving the hospital. Most are considered preventable.

What Can Lead to a Return

Medication confusion
Falls and reduced mobility
Poor nutrition or dehydration
Changes in condition or infections
Overwhelm at home

How Jane's Helps

Reinforce medication routines and schedules
Supervision, safe transfers, mobility support
Healthy meals and hydration reminders
Attention to warning signs, clear protocols
Respite care, open communication, flexibility
