Safe Return Home

Thoughtful Support After a Hospital Stay

Nearly 1 in 5 patients experience an adverse event within 3 weeks of leaving the hospital. Most are considered preventable.

What Can Lead to a Return

• Medication confusion
• Falls and reduced mobility
• Poor nutrition or dehydration
• Changes in condition or infections
• Overwhelm at home

How Jane's Helps

✓ Reinforce medication routines and schedules
✓ Supervision, safe transfers, mobility support
✓ Healthy meals and hydration reminders
✓ Attention to warning signs, clear protocols
✓ Respite care, open communication, flexibility

Home care is an integral part of the ongoing treatment plan.

Memory Care

Dementia & Alzheimer's Care

When memory changes, the whole family feels it. What once felt simple can become heavy. Routines change, safety becomes a concern, and families are often carrying more than anyone sees.

What Families May Face

• Confusion and memory loss
• Wandering or safety concerns
• Agitation or anxiety
• Disrupted routines
• Family overwhelm

How Jane's Helps

✓ Calm, structured daily support
✓ Consistent daily routines
✓ Dementia-trained caregivers
✓ Safety monitoring
✓ Ongoing family communication
